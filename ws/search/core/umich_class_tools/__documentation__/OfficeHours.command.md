### Office Hours (`oh`)
*Usage:* `oh [class]`
*Aliases:* `office-hours`, `office_hours`, `office`

If `class` is empty, will redirect to the EECS Office Hours home page at https://oh.eecs.umich.edu.

The following classes are supported:

- EECS 280
- EECS 281
- EECS 398
- EECS 482
- EECS 484
- EECS 485
- EECS 489
- EECS 490
- EECS 498
- ENGR 101
- ENGR 151
